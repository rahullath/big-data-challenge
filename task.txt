Introduction
• Predicting stock returns is a fundamental problem in financial
analytics.
• Traditional linear models often struggle to capture complex
relationships in financial data, while modern machine learning
techniques can model nonlinear patterns more effectively.
• This assignment explores and compares three approaches:
• LASSO Regression (Linear + Regularization)
• Random Forest (Ensemble Learning)
• Artificial Neural Networks
• SVM
• The goal is to evaluate their performance on the financial
dataset and understand their strengths and limitations.
